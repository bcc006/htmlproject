bookSwap is a site to make trading books on campus with other students, easier and more convenient.
Students will be able to use this site to search for a book on their campus, see if another student is currently
selling it, and then get in contact with that student selling it. Its that Easy!

Simply go into your user account page, and in the center of the page there should be a button labeled, "Upload a book".
When you click on that you will be directed to a page that asks for more details on the book you are trying to upload!

Your personal account page should have a button that allows the user to upload a picture from their own computer documents.
